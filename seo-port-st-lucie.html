The city is home to the New York Mets and St. Lucie County Spring Training Complex, which brings 100,000+ spring training baseball fans and families annually (February-April). Spring training creates massive spikes in hotel, restaurant, entertainment, and attraction searches. Meanwhile, Port St. Lucie is one of America's fastest-growing retirement communities, with an estimated 60,000+ residents over 65. This creates demand for retirement-focused services, healthcare, assisted living, active adult communities, and senior-oriented entertainment. Yet simultaneously, Port St. Lucie attracts young families seeking Florida beachside living without the Miami/Miami Beach crush, creating "family relocation" searches alongside "retirement community" searches.
