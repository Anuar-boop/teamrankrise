It's a major employer hub driven by University of Tennessee, Oak Ridge National Laboratory, and TVA headquarters. It's a growing tech and startup scene attracting young talent. And it's a city with distinct neighborhoods — Downtown, Old City, Market Square, Bearden, West Knoxville, Farragut, Maryville, Powell, and Halls — each with their own character and search demands.
