The city's port operations create shipping, logistics, and maritime commerce. Military installations (Fort Stewart, Hunter Army Airfield) create military employment and family searches. Tourism searches dominate — visitors seek historic sites, squares, dining, entertainment, and cultural experiences. Unlike typical cities, Savannah's economy blends tourism, military, creative education, and port commerce in a historically-preserved setting.
