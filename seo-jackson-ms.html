Student Population Creates Seasonal Demand Spikes and Shifting Demographics

15,000+ students at Jackson State, Tougaloo, and Millsaps create massive seasonal swings in demand for housing, dining, entertainment, tutoring, and services. August-September (back-to-school) and May-June (end-of-year) create predictable search volume spikes.
