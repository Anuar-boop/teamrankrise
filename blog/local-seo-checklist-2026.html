The Complete Local SEO Checklist for 2026

AlexFeb 17, 202616 min read

This is the checklist we use for every new client at RankRise. It covers everything from the basics to advanced tactics — organized by category so you can work through it systematically.

Print it out. Bookmark it. Work through it one section at a time. Every item you complete moves you closer to page 1.

Google Business Profile (14 Items)

Your Google Business Profile is the foundation of local SEO. If you do nothing else, do this section.

  • ☐ Claim and verify your GBP listing
  • ☐ Choose the most specific primary category — read our categories guide
  • ☐ Add all relevant secondary categories (up to 10)
  • ☐ Write a complete 750-character business description — see our description guide
  • ☐ Verify business name matches your real legal name (no keyword stuffing)
  • ☐ Set accurate business hours (including holiday hours)
  • ☐ Add a local phone number (not a toll-free 800 number)
  • ☐ Link to your best landing page (not just the homepage)
  • ☐ Upload 10+ photos — exterior, interior, team, and work examples
  • ☐ List all services/products with descriptions and prices
  • ☐ Define your service area accurately (service-area businesses)
  • ☐ Enable messaging (if you can respond within 24 hours)
  • ☐ Add your opening date
  • ☐ Set up appointment booking link (if applicable)

GBP Ongoing Activities (6 Items)

  • ☐ Post weekly updates — GBP posting guide
  • ☐ Add new photos monthly (at minimum)
  • ☐ Respond to every review within 48 hours — response templates
  • ☐ Answer all Q&A questions
  • ☐ Update hours for holidays and special events
  • ☐ Check for and report fake/spam competitors

Reviews (7 Items)

Reviews are the second most important ranking factor for Google Maps rankings.

  • ☐ Create a direct Google review link and save it
  • ☐ Train staff to ask every customer for a review
  • ☐ Set up automated review request emails/texts (within 24 hours of service)
  • ☐ Add review QR code to invoices, receipts, and business cards
  • ☐ Respond to all positive reviews with keyword-rich replies
  • ☐ Respond to all negative reviews professionally — negative review guide
  • ☐ Target 50+ reviews as your first milestone — first 50 reviews guide

On-Page SEO (10 Items)

Your website needs to reinforce what your GBP tells Google.

  • ☐ Include city + service in every page title tag — "Plumber in Dallas, TX | Joe's Plumbing"
  • ☐ Write unique meta descriptions for every page (with city name)
  • ☐ Use H1 tags with your primary keyword + city
  • ☐ Create individual service pages (not one generic "Services" page) — service page guide
  • ☐ Create location pages for each city you serve — multi-location guide
  • ☐ Add NAP (name, address, phone) to your footer or contact page
  • ☐ Embed a Google Map on your contact page
  • ☐ Mention neighborhoods, landmarks, and local details in your content
  • ☐ Use internal links between related pages — internal linking guide
  • ☐ Write content targeting local keywords — keyword research guide

Technical SEO (8 Items)

  • ☐ Ensure your site loads in under 3 seconds — speed optimization guide
  • ☐ Pass Google's mobile-friendly test — mobile optimization guide
  • ☐ Install an SSL certificate (HTTPS) — non-negotiable in 2026
  • ☐ Add LocalBusiness schema markup — schema guide
  • ☐ Submit an XML sitemap to Google Search Console — GSC guide
  • ☐ Fix all broken links (404 errors)
  • ☐ Set up canonical tags to prevent duplicate content — duplicate content guide
  • ☐ Ensure proper indexing — not showing on Google?

Citations & Directories (6 Items)

  • ☐ Submit to top citation sources — citations guide
  • ☐ Ensure 100% NAP consistency across all listings — NAP guide
  • ☐ Claim Apple Maps listing (mapsconnect.apple.com)
  • ☐ Claim Bing Places listing (bingplaces.com)
  • ☐ Submit to industry-specific directories (Healthgrades, Avvo, HomeAdvisor, etc.)
  • ☐ Get listed on your local Chamber of Commerce

Link Building (5 Items)

  • ☐ Get backlinks from local organizations — backlink guide
  • ☐ Sponsor local events for .org backlinks
  • ☐ Join your Chamber of Commerce (directory link)
  • ☐ Guest post on local blogs or news sites
  • ☐ Build relationships with complementary businesses for cross-linking

Content Strategy (5 Items)

  • ☐ Create a content calendar — content calendar guide
  • ☐ Write blog posts targeting local keywords — content writing guide
  • ☐ Create FAQ content targeting "near me" searches — near-me guide
  • ☐ Optimize for voice search — voice search guide
  • ☐ Build trust signals on your website — trust-building guide

Tracking & Analytics (5 Items)

  • ☐ Set up Google Search Console — setup guide
  • ☐ Set up Google Analytics — analytics guide
  • ☐ Track your Google Maps rankings weekly
  • ☐ Monitor GBP Insights monthly
  • ☐ Track calls, form submissions, and direction requests — tracking guide

How to Use This Checklist

Don't try to do everything at once. Here's the priority order:

  1. Week 1: Google Business Profile (all 14 items)
  2. Week 2: On-Page SEO (10 items) + Technical SEO (8 items)
  3. Week 3: Citations (6 items) + Reviews (start the process)
  4. Week 4: Content strategy + Link building (begin outreach)
  5. Ongoing: GBP activities, reviews, content, tracking

Most businesses can complete the one-time items in 2-4 weeks. The ongoing activities are what separate businesses that rank #1 from everyone else.
